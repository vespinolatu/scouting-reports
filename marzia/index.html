Technical / Tactical

  • Positions herself consistently in free central pockets, offering passing lanes and supporting circulation.
  • Displays strong give-and-go habits: after releasing the ball, immediately repositions to re-offer and remain involved.
  • Willing to carry the ball forward when space opens, attacking central channels although control and direction under pressure can be refined.
  • Defensive decision-making is inconsistent; often steps towards the ball-carrier too aggressively, leaving space behind her.
  • Defensive approach is direct rather than controlling; prioritises winning the ball immediately instead of managing space and timing.
  • Does not scan frequently enough before reception, limiting her awareness of pressure from behind.
  • Can lose awareness of attackers arriving from her blind side due to limited scanning.

Summary

21-year-old central midfielder for Internacional RS, operating mainly as an interior in a midfield three. High-involvement profile supported by a heatmap heavily concentrated in central zones, indicating constant availability in both possession and defensive phases.

Reliable ball-circulating midfielder who consistently finds free central spaces to support build-up and maintain continuity. Radar data confirms strong short-range pass completion.

Out of possession, plays with notable intensity and commitment. Actively steps out to engage the ball carrier and sustains high effort levels across 90 minutes. However, defensive awareness and timing require refinement: can lose track of runners behind her and commits too directly without adjusting body orientation to delay or control actions.

Scanning habits are underdeveloped for her role. Limited shoulder checks before receiving and when defending reduce her ability to anticipate pressure and manage space efficiently.

Profiles as a high-intensity, possession-supporting interior midfielder, best suited to structured, high-tempo systems that value work rate, availability and midfield coverage over creativity or tempo control. Development focus lies in scanning, defensive timing and efficiency in decision-making.
